How prescription orders work

Products marked Rx (isotretinoin, finasteride, clobetasol, itraconazole and similar) are Schedule H medicines. Add them to your cart like any other product — at checkout you'll attach a photo of a valid prescription, and our pharmacist verifies it before dispatch. No prescription yet? Consult your dermatologist first; these medicines need medical supervision.
